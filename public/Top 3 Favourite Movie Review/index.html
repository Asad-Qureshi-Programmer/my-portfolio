Top Movies (According to Asad)

My Top 3 Favourite Anime Movies

1) Your Name

Director- Makoto Shinkai

This anime movie makes non-anime viewers fall in love with anime movies. It's perfect blend of all the emotions. This story depicts "Love in Destiny" very beautifully.

2) Suzume no Tojimari

Director- Makoto Shinkai

Like always, Makoto Shinkai movies has the best quality. The realistic anime with mixture of emotions, actions and suspense is what makes it my 2nd favourite anime movie.

3) Weathering With you

Director- Makoto Shinkai

Completely Opposite to "Your name", here the lovers fight for their love, even though it's not in their destiny. Very Emotional as well as Heart Touching Story


My Top 3 Favourite Movies

1) Avengers: End Game

Director- Anthony Russo, Joe Russo

The best Marvel Studio's Movie till now. This is the best movie which links to most of the past movies of MCU. All the Main Characters in all MCU movies were there and it's full of Action, VFX, Great Storyline, Suspense, Thriller and many more

2) Justice League

Director- Zack Snyder

DC is famous for it's Dark movies, with dark Storyline and Zack Snyder has proved no one can direct this movie better than him. No words to explain it, it's just too good.

3) KGF (Kolar Gold Fields)

Director- Prashanth Neel

It's an Indian Movie among Hollywood Movies, which I like the most. It's a Dark Movie as well as Dark storyline which make me like it.
